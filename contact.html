Frequently Asked Questions

Is Word Counter really free?

Yes! Word Counter is 100% free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium features. You can use all features unlimited times without any restrictions.

Do you store my text?

No. Your privacy is our top priority. All text processing happens in your browser, and we never store, analyze, or transmit your content to our servers.

Can I use Word Counter offline?

Once the page is loaded, the basic counting functionality works without an internet connection. However, an internet connection is required for initial page load.

How accurate is the word count?

Our algorithm provides 100% accurate word counting following standard word counting rules. We count words separated by spaces and handle various text formats correctly.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Use the contact form above to share your ideas, and we'll consider them for future updates.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, Word Counter is web-based and fully responsive, working perfectly on all devices. We're exploring mobile app options for the future.
