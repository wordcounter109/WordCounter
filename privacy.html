Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Last Updated: December 7, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Word Counter. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our word counting tool and website. By using Word Counter, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Text Input Data

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any text you enter into our word counter tool. All text processing happens entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. Your text never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This information is collected through standard server logs and is used solely to improve our service and understand how users interact with our website.

1.3 Cookies and Local Storage

Word Counter uses minimal cookies and browser local storage for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Local Storage: Used to save your text temporarily (auto-save feature) on your device only. This data is stored locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use analytics services to understand website usage patterns. These cookies do not identify you personally.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our website.

2. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used for:

  • Providing and maintaining our word counting service
  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues
  • Protecting against abuse and malicious activity
  • Communicating with you if you contact us through our contact form

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 No Text Storage

We do NOT store any text you input into our word counter. All text processing occurs in your browser, and your content remains completely private and confidential.

3.2 Local Storage

If you use our auto-save feature, text is temporarily stored in your browser's local storage. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to our servers
  • Can be cleared by you at any time
  • Is automatically deleted when you clear your browser data

3.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect our website and any information we collect:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Limited data retention policies

4. Third-Party Services

Word Counter may use the following third-party services:

4.1 Analytics Services

We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our website. These services use cookies and collect anonymized data about your visit. They do not access or store any text you enter into our word counter.

4.2 Content Delivery Networks (CDN)

We use CDN services to deliver website resources (CSS, JavaScript libraries, fonts) quickly and reliably. These services may collect standard technical information about your connection.

4.3 No Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information with third parties except as necessary to provide our service or as required by law.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your privacy:

  • Access: You can access any information we may have collected about you
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of any data we hold
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of analytics cookies through browser settings
  • Control: You control your browser's local storage and can clear it anytime
  • Privacy Tools: You can use browser privacy features like "Do Not Track"

6. Children's Privacy

Word Counter is appropriate for users of all ages, including students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our tool processes text locally in the browser without collecting or storing any user data, making it safe for children to use under parental supervision.

7. International Users

Word Counter is accessible worldwide. If you access our service from outside your country, please note that minimal technical data may be transmitted internationally. However, no text content you enter is ever transmitted or stored.

8. Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store your text input, there is no retention period for your content. Technical logs and analytics data are retained for a limited time (typically 90 days) and then automatically deleted.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your privacy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@wordcounter.com
  • Contact Form: Visit our Contact Page
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 24-48 hours

11. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided above.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Summary

In short: Word Counter is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or share your text. All counting happens in your browser. We collect minimal technical data necessary to operate the website and improve our service. Your privacy and trust are paramount to us.
